I'm Oscar, I'm a Software Engineer from Bolivia living in Munich since 2016.

When I was a kid I cannot tell you how many times I broke my computer and had to take it to the repair shop, even then I was more into games than actual programming, this was the time when a pentium (I) computer with 32mbs of RAM costed over 2000$us, that was a ton of money specially for Bolivia.

After I finished school (I was at a German school, funny uh?), I actually started studying Finance Engineering, which was just a fancy title for an accountant career, also due to my mothers insistance I was enrolled in a very expensive university, after one year I got tired of (all of) it and finally my love for computers came through, so I switched to the state university and started studying something close to Computer Science.

During the college years I did a lot of competitive programming, my career was supposed to last 5 years I finished in 4 and also graduated with honors (I wanted to make up the time I lost), I started working for an american offshoring company, made some good (and very talented) friends but soon realized that management didn't care about the people in the company (country?), so I started freelancing, then I had a bunch of German customers and eventually ended up moving to Munich!

I released an app for pilots in south america: TPLatino, also trying to build Strest, as well as some contributions to OSS, ex: link-preview-js

Every once in a while I feel the need to write stuff down, they are mostly high level thoughts with some sprinkle of technical, I mostly prefer to write on the high level stuff, because I believe most of the time (which) technology you pick has little importance, nobody cares about the programming language or the tech stack, as long as it solves a problem, I could program in COBOL or PHP for all I care, as long as the solution works, everyone will be happy.

I do however have more love for certain technologies, so here are some core beliefs:

  • Typescript > Javascript
  • Haskell > Rust > Scala > Everything else
  • NestJS > Springboot > Spring
  • Native Mobile > React-Native > W/e is left here (ionic?)
  • Redux > Mobx > Context > Hooks

I would have to include a lot more technologies/platforms/SaaS to be exhaustive, but there is no need, I've pretty much done/touched everything under the sun (with the exception of some advanced stuff, example AI, which I believe is a scam anyways)
